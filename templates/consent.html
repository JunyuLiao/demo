High-Dimensional Regret Minimization

an interactive system for finding your favorite option among a large database

User Study Information

You are invited to participate in a research study to evaluate the effectiveness of our interactive recommendation algorithm. This study aims to understand how users interact with high-dimensional preference learning systems.

What you will do:

  • Use an interactive algorithm to find your preferred options from a database of cars
  • Answer a number of questions by choosing between two options
  • Rate the performance of the algorithm
  • The process typically takes less than 3 minutes depending on your preferences

Your Participation

Voluntary Participation: Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

No Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information that could identify you. Your responses are anonymous.

Research Purpose: The data collected will be used solely for research purposes to improve interactive decision-making algorithms.

Privacy & Data Protection

No Data Storage: Your responses are not saved or stored on our servers. All data is processed locally in your browser.

Anonymous Usage: We only collect anonymous usage statistics (such as number of questions answered) for research analysis.

Secure Connection: All communication is encrypted and secure.

How to Participate

  1. Click "Agree and Start" to begin the study
  2. You will see two car options with different specifications
  3. Choose the option you prefer by clicking on it or entering your choice
  4. Continue answering questions until the algorithm finds your preferred options
  5. You can stop early at any time using the "Stop & Get Results" button
  6. Review your final recommendations when the process is complete

This study is conducted for research purposes. By clicking "Agree and Start", you acknowledge that you have read and understood the information above.
